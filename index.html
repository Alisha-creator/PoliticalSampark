Who We are

Welcome to Political Sampark

Political Sampark is an emerging platform where young, enthusiastic, and experienced professionals engaged in political-administrative practices. We work together in the pre-election and post-election roles of political parties, candidates, and eminent personalities and we get involved in their political projects.

We are motivated by the idea that holistic and inclusive development happens when youth participates in large-scale political administration. The motive of Political Sampark is to establish a transparent system in the interest of public education, health care, and infrastructure. We believe that public education should be delivered in the broadest public sense.
